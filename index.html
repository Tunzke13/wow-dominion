in this opening chapter of the battle for azeroth saga

Get back to fight and reclaim azeroth.

Garrosh has now full grip over kalimdor and the eastern kingdoms. His expantionist and extrimist goals brought ruins to many lives of azeroth. As he sought to end his foes once and for all, it's up to the champions of azeroth to defy the warmongering warchief of the true horde. All oppressed factions must put down its differences and take arms to end Garrosh's tyranny.

Features

Skirmish

A small size solo dungeon content, you will be accompanied by three A.I to fight along side you and upgrade them to improve their combat effectiveness. Win the battle and earn rewards.

New Dungeons and Raids

Seven new dungeons at start, and one challenging raid. Rally champions of azeroth, end the tyranny.

Warfronts

Garrosh will not let your war efforts unchallenged, he will retalliate and you must defend the captured bases. Defend and earn rewards.
